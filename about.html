IEEE Ltd. is an electrical solutions specialist firm that offers customers reliable and long-lasting electrical panels, gear switches and distribution boards across the West African Sub-Region. As a renowned business enterprise with international connections and partnerships with reputable equipment manufacturers, we set the pace as a reliable, trustworthy and competent enterprise in electrical solutions and services delivery. Our experienced engineers are professionally certified and grounded electrical solutions and panel builders. We guarantee 100% commitment to your specific requirements at an affordable rate without compromising safety, quality and timely delivery. Our uniqueness as a dedicated electrical solutions specialist firm is drawn from our specially equipped facilities, which also serve as training workshops for electrical interns and support personnel, and are actively supported by our original equipment manufacturers – Hager & Legrand. For best global practices, we pride ourselves on adhering to stringent testing guidelines which ensure that we build and deliver electrical panels and solutions; irrespective of size and location of use. .
